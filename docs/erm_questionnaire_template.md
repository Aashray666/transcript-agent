# ERM Pre-Call Structured Questionnaire

## Purpose

This questionnaire is completed by the client BEFORE the CRO interview. It provides the structured factual foundation that the Knowledge Summarizer Agent uses to contextualize each risk during scoring. The interview captures qualitative judgment and narrative; this questionnaire captures quantitative facts and organizational context.

## Design Principles

1. **Multi-parameter extraction:** Each question is designed to elicit multiple data points from a single answer
2. **Domain coverage:** Spans all ERM-relevant domains — strategy, operations, finance, regulatory, technology, people, reputation, ESG
3. **Scoring-relevant:** Every question maps to at least one input needed by the Scoring Agent
4. **Sector-aware:** Base template with automotive-specific additions
5. **Efficient:** Under 75 questions total, grouped by domain for logical flow

## Section A: Company Profile & Strategy (Questions 1-12)

These establish the baseline context for ALL risk scoring — sector, size, geography, strategic direction.

**A1.** Company legal name, headquarters location, and year of incorporation.
> *Extracts: Entity identity, jurisdiction, maturity*

**A2.** Primary industry sector and sub-sector (e.g., Automotive — OEM / Tier-1 Supplier / Tier-2 Supplier / Aftermarket).
> *Extracts: Sector classification, value chain position*

**A3.** Annual revenue (most recent fiscal year) and revenue trend over the last 3 years (growing / stable / declining). Provide currency.
> *Extracts: Company size, growth trajectory, currency exposure baseline*

**A4.** EBITDA margin (most recent fiscal year) and trend over the last 3 years.
> *Extracts: Profitability baseline for financial impact calibration*

**A5.** Total number of employees (full-time equivalent) and approximate split by function (manufacturing / engineering / corporate / sales & distribution).
> *Extracts: Workforce size, operational complexity, labour concentration*

**A6.** List all countries/regions where you have manufacturing operations, R&D centres, and significant sales presence.
> *Extracts: Geographic footprint, regulatory jurisdiction exposure, geopolitical exposure, currency exposure*

**A7.** Describe your current strategic priorities for the next 3-5 years (list top 5).
> *Extracts: Strategic direction, transformation risks, investment priorities*

**A8.** What major capital investment programmes are currently underway or planned? Include approximate total investment value and timeline.
> *Extracts: Capital commitment, execution risk exposure, financial strain indicators*

**A9.** What percentage of your revenue comes from each of the following: (a) Internal combustion engine vehicles, (b) Electric/hybrid vehicles, (c) Parts & aftermarket, (d) Financial services, (e) Other.
> *Extracts: Revenue mix, transition exposure, stranded asset risk baseline*

**A10.** Do you have any joint ventures, strategic alliances, or significant minority investments? If yes, list the partner, geography, purpose, and your ownership percentage.
> *Extracts: JV/partnership exposure, counterparty risk, governance complexity*

**A11.** What is your current credit rating (if rated)? Has it changed in the last 2 years?
> *Extracts: Financial health indicator, capital access risk*

**A12.** What is your current net debt / EBITDA ratio? What is your target range?
> *Extracts: Leverage, financial flexibility, refinancing risk*

## Section B: Operations & Supply Chain (Questions 13-25)

These inform operational impact scoring and supply chain likelihood assessment.

**B13.** How many manufacturing plants do you operate? List locations and primary product lines per plant.
> *Extracts: Plant count, geographic concentration, product-plant mapping*

**B14.** What is your current average capacity utilisation across plants?
> *Extracts: Operational headroom, demand risk sensitivity*

**B15.** Describe your production model: (a) Make-to-order, (b) Make-to-stock, (c) Hybrid. What is your typical order-to-delivery lead time?
> *Extracts: Inventory risk profile, demand responsiveness*

**B16.** List your top 5 suppliers by spend. For each, indicate: component/material supplied, geographic location of their primary facility, whether you have an alternative source, and approximate % of your total procurement spend.
> *Extracts: Supplier concentration, single-source risk, geographic supply chain exposure*

**B17.** What percentage of your critical components are single-sourced (no qualified alternative supplier)?
> *Extracts: Supply chain concentration severity*

**B18.** What raw materials represent your largest cost exposure? List top 5 with approximate annual spend.
> *Extracts: Commodity exposure, hedging needs, cost structure vulnerability*

**B19.** Do you have formal hedging programmes for commodities and/or currencies? If yes, what is the typical hedge horizon (months)?
> *Extracts: Financial risk mitigation maturity, residual exposure window*

**B20.** Have you experienced any significant supply chain disruptions in the last 3 years? If yes, describe the event, duration, and estimated financial impact.
> *Extracts: Historical frequency data for supply chain likelihood, incident severity calibration*

**B21.** Describe your logistics model: (a) Primary modes of transport (road/rail/sea/air), (b) Key port or hub dependencies, (c) Just-in-time vs. buffer stock approach.
> *Extracts: Logistics vulnerability, JIT risk, infrastructure dependency*

**B22.** What is your average inventory holding (days of cover) for critical components?
> *Extracts: Buffer capacity, disruption resilience window*

**B23.** Do you have formal business continuity plans (BCPs) for your manufacturing operations? Have they been tested in the last 12 months?
> *Extracts: Control maturity for operational risks, tested vs. theoretical controls*

**B24.** What is your current product recall history over the last 5 years? List number of recalls, approximate vehicles affected, and root cause category (design / manufacturing / software / supplier).
> *Extracts: Historical frequency for product safety likelihood, root cause patterns*

**B25.** What is your current warranty provision as a percentage of revenue?
> *Extracts: Quality risk baseline, financial exposure from product issues*

## Section C: Technology & Cyber (Questions 26-35)

These inform technology impact scoring and cyber likelihood assessment.

**C26.** Describe your IT/OT landscape: (a) Age of core ERP system, (b) Age of manufacturing execution systems (MES), (c) Number of legacy systems (>10 years old) still in production use.
> *Extracts: Legacy system risk, technology debt, modernisation urgency*

**C27.** How many connected/software-defined vehicles are currently in your active fleet (sold and on the road)?
> *Extracts: Connected vehicle attack surface, cyber risk scale*

**C28.** Do your vehicles support over-the-air (OTA) software updates? If yes, what percentage of your fleet?
> *Extracts: OTA risk exposure, software update failure surface*

**C29.** Have you experienced any cybersecurity incidents (including near-misses) in the last 3 years? If yes, describe the type (ransomware / data breach / OT intrusion / other), impact, and resolution time.
> *Extracts: Historical frequency for cyber likelihood, incident type patterns*

**C30.** Do you have a dedicated cybersecurity function? What is the approximate headcount?
> *Extracts: Cyber control maturity, resource adequacy*

**C31.** Have you conducted a cybersecurity penetration test or red team exercise in the last 12 months? Scope (IT only / OT included / connected vehicle included)?
> *Extracts: Control testing maturity, coverage gaps*

**C32.** Do you have a formal cyber incident response plan? Has it been tested via tabletop exercise or simulation in the last 12 months?
> *Extracts: Response readiness, tested vs. theoretical controls*

**C33.** What is your current annual cybersecurity spend as a percentage of IT budget?
> *Extracts: Investment adequacy indicator*

**C34.** Are you currently undertaking any major technology transformation programmes (cloud migration, ERP replacement, digital twin, etc.)? Describe scope and timeline.
> *Extracts: Technology change risk, execution risk*

**C35.** What percentage of your software development for vehicle systems is done in-house vs. outsourced to third parties?
> *Extracts: Software dependency, third-party technology risk*

## Section D: Regulatory & Compliance (Questions 36-45)

These inform regulatory impact scoring and compliance likelihood assessment.

**D36.** List the primary regulatory bodies that oversee your operations in each major jurisdiction (e.g., NHTSA, EU Type Approval, UNECE).
> *Extracts: Regulatory landscape, jurisdiction count, compliance complexity*

**D37.** What emissions standards are you currently required to comply with? List by market (e.g., Euro 7, US EPA Tier 3, China 6b).
> *Extracts: Emissions compliance exposure, standard-specific risk*

**D38.** What is your current fleet average CO2 emissions (g/km) vs. the regulatory target in your largest market?
> *Extracts: Compliance gap severity, penalty exposure*

**D39.** Have you received any regulatory fines, penalties, or enforcement actions in the last 3 years? If yes, describe the nature and amount.
> *Extracts: Historical frequency for regulatory likelihood, severity calibration*

**D40.** Are you subject to mandatory sustainability/ESG reporting requirements (e.g., CSRD, TCFD)? What is your current readiness level (fully ready / partially ready / not ready)?
> *Extracts: ESG compliance risk, data readiness gap*

**D41.** Do you hold any certifications critical to your operations (ISO 9001, IATF 16949, ISO 14001, ISO 27001)? List all and their current status (active / under review / at risk).
> *Extracts: Certification risk, quality system maturity*

**D42.** Are there any pending or anticipated regulatory changes in your key markets that could materially affect your operations or product lineup in the next 24 months?
> *Extracts: Forward-looking regulatory risk, policy uncertainty*

**D43.** What is your current data privacy compliance posture? Do you collect data from connected vehicles? In which jurisdictions?
> *Extracts: Data privacy risk, cross-border data exposure*

**D44.** Do you have a formal regulatory change monitoring process? How frequently is it reviewed?
> *Extracts: Regulatory control maturity*

**D45.** Have you been involved in any product liability litigation in the last 5 years? If yes, describe the nature and current status.
> *Extracts: Litigation history, liability exposure*

## Section E: Financial & Market (Questions 46-53)

These inform financial impact scoring and market risk likelihood.

**E46.** What are your top 5 currencies by revenue exposure? What percentage of revenue is in each?
> *Extracts: Currency concentration, FX risk mapping*

**E47.** What percentage of your cost base is denominated in a different currency from your revenue?
> *Extracts: Natural hedge gap, FX mismatch severity*

**E48.** What is your current total capital expenditure plan for the next 3 years? What percentage is committed vs. discretionary?
> *Extracts: Capital commitment rigidity, financial flexibility*

**E49.** Who are your top 5 customers by revenue? What percentage of total revenue does each represent?
> *Extracts: Customer concentration, revenue risk*

**E50.** What is your current market share in your top 3 markets? Has it changed in the last 2 years?
> *Extracts: Competitive position, market share trend*

**E51.** What is your current order book visibility (months of confirmed orders)?
> *Extracts: Demand certainty, revenue risk horizon*

**E52.** Do you have any significant debt maturities in the next 24 months? If yes, approximate amount and timing.
> *Extracts: Refinancing risk, liquidity pressure*

**E53.** What is your current insurance coverage for key risks (property, product liability, cyber, business interruption)? Are there any known coverage gaps?
> *Extracts: Risk transfer maturity, residual exposure*

## Section F: People, Health & Safety (Questions 54-61)

These inform people impact scoring and workforce risk likelihood.

**F54.** What percentage of your workforce is unionised? In which countries?
> *Extracts: Labour relations risk, industrial action likelihood*

**F55.** Have you experienced any industrial action (strikes, work stoppages) in the last 3 years? If yes, describe duration and impact.
> *Extracts: Historical frequency for labour risk likelihood*

**F56.** What is your current Lost Time Injury Frequency Rate (LTIFR)?
> *Extracts: Safety performance baseline, H&S risk calibration*

**F57.** What is your current voluntary employee turnover rate? Is it above or below industry average?
> *Extracts: Talent retention risk, workforce stability*

**F58.** Do you have any active workforce restructuring or transformation programmes? If yes, describe scope (number of roles affected, timeline, geographies).
> *Extracts: Workforce transformation risk, labour relations pressure*

**F59.** What are your most critical skill gaps currently? List top 3 roles/capabilities you are struggling to fill.
> *Extracts: Talent risk, capability gaps*

**F60.** Have you had any workplace fatalities or serious injuries in the last 5 years?
> *Extracts: Safety incident history, severity calibration*

**F61.** Do you use contract/temporary labour in manufacturing? What percentage of your manufacturing workforce?
> *Extracts: Contractor risk, workforce flexibility vs. quality trade-off*

## Section G: Reputation & ESG (Questions 62-68)

These inform reputation impact scoring and ESG risk likelihood.

**G62.** Have you experienced any significant negative media coverage in the last 3 years? If yes, describe the topic and duration.
> *Extracts: Reputational incident history, media risk calibration*

**G63.** What is your current ESG rating (if rated)? From which agency? Has it changed in the last 2 years?
> *Extracts: ESG performance baseline, rating trajectory*

**G64.** What are your published sustainability targets (carbon neutrality date, emissions reduction targets, circular economy commitments)?
> *Extracts: ESG commitment exposure, greenwashing risk*

**G65.** What percentage of your energy consumption comes from renewable sources?
> *Extracts: Energy transition progress, carbon risk exposure*

**G66.** Do you have a formal whistleblower / ethics reporting mechanism? How many reports were received in the last 12 months?
> *Extracts: Ethics control maturity, misconduct risk indicator*

**G67.** Have you conducted a formal materiality assessment for ESG topics in the last 2 years?
> *Extracts: ESG governance maturity*

**G68.** What is your current Scope 1, 2, and 3 carbon emissions (if measured)?
> *Extracts: Carbon exposure quantification, regulatory risk baseline*

## Section H: Governance & Risk Management Maturity (Questions 69-75)

These inform control maturity assessment across ALL risks — critical for likelihood adjustment.

**H69.** Describe your enterprise risk management framework. Is it aligned to a standard (ISO 31000, COSO ERM)?
> *Extracts: ERM maturity, framework alignment*

**H70.** How frequently does your board review the enterprise risk register?
> *Extracts: Governance maturity, risk oversight frequency*

**H71.** Do you have a dedicated Chief Risk Officer or equivalent? What is the reporting line?
> *Extracts: Risk function maturity, organizational priority*

**H72.** How many risks are currently on your formal enterprise risk register?
> *Extracts: Register comprehensiveness, potential blind spots*

**H73.** When was your last formal enterprise risk assessment conducted?
> *Extracts: Assessment currency, staleness risk*

**H74.** Do you conduct scenario analysis or stress testing for your top risks? If yes, how frequently and for which risks?
> *Extracts: Risk quantification maturity, preparedness*

**H75.** Do you have a formal crisis management plan? Has it been tested in the last 12 months? Describe the scope of the last test.
> *Extracts: Crisis readiness, response capability*
